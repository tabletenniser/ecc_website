About Us


The University of Toronto Engineering Chinese Club (ECC) is a student organization and its mission is to "Unite Chinese engineers and empower all engineers". ECC wishes to enrich student life and their undergrad experience through various academic, athletic and networking events. For this upcoming year, ECC will also aim to promote local outreach and charity events to its members so the Chinese engineers can have many opportunities to interact with the local communities and truly experience the Canadian culture. ECC have hosted a number of successful academic events to broaden student's knowledge about Professional Experience Year Internship Program and Graduate School by learning from their fellow students' personal experiences. ECC has also hosted sports tournaments to promote healthy and active lifestyle within the student community. Last but not least, our Alumni Dinner and Mentorship Program provided excellent networking opportunities for all members.

 

After running for almost 8 years, we have approximately 300 members in the undergraduate level and close to 400 alumni at the most reputable university in Canada. One of the factors that make ECC such a close community is that our Alumni always try to stay connected with ECC and always help the club to grow and succeed in any way they can. For more stories about our alumni, please visit the Alumni page.

 

多大华人工程社（ECC---Engineering Chinese Club）创办于2004年，现已发展成为多伦多大学最大的非盈利性学生组织之一。多大华人工程社一直致力于帮助所有多大的工程学子，以提供学术，社交和充实课余生活的各类活动为宗旨。每年，ECC会开展学术交流活动，以帮助会员充分了解PEY和研究生课程的信息。同时，ECC所组织的社交活动为每位多大的工程学子提供了熟知加国文化，了解企业运作的机会。创立至今，ECC与多个学生社团和企业携手，相互汲取和合作。通过一系列活动的举办，多大华人工程社已在多大学者，学生，以及社会各界人士中树立了良好的口碑和评价。昔日的成绩并没有阻碍多大华人工程社的锐意进取，新一年度的各项活动已在紧锣密鼓的筹备之中。而一直以来，ECC所有的执委都为多大华人工程社的发展做出了不可磨灭的贡献，未来，多大华人工程社也期待吸纳更多优秀的多大学子。时间静静流转，我们一路成长,一路收获。愿多大华人工程社陪伴每一位多大学子一同分享喜悦，收获成功。
