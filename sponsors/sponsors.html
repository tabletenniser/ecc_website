Are You Looking to Immigrate to Canada?

 

Do you wish to remain in Canada after you graduate? The most effective and efficient method to do this is via the Canadian Experience Class (CEC) immigration program. This program allows foreign students of Canadian universities preferential eligibility for permanent residence if they meet the following criteria. You must have:

  • studied at least two years at a Canadian university, and
  • at least one year of authorized work experience in Canada following graduation in a professional, managerial or skilled worker occupation
